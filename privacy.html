Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide when creating an account, including your name and email address. We also collect data about your gym check-ins, pod activity, and app usage to provide the GymPact service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to operate GymPact, display your progress to pod members, calculate leaderboard rankings, send push notifications about pod activity, and improve the app experience.

3. Information Shared With Others

Your name and check-in activity is visible to members of your pod. Your overall ranking may appear on the global leaderboard if you have enabled this in settings. We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, hosted on AWS infrastructure. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

5. Push Notifications

With your permission, we send push notifications about pod check-ins, streak reminders, and weekly summaries. You can disable these at any time in your device settings or within the app.

6. Your Rights

You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen. This permanently removes all your data from our systems. You can also request a copy of your data by contacting us.

7. Children's Privacy

GymPact is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at gympact.support@gmail.com
